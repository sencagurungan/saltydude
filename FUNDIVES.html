Top Dive Sites in Anilao, Batangas

Beautiful Coral Reefs & Marine Sanctuaries

- Cathedral Rock – A famous underwater sanctuary with two giant rock formations and lots of friendly fish.

- Sombrero Island – Named after its hat-like shape, this site features colorful coral gardens and sea turtles.

- Twin Rocks – A must-visit site with shallow reefs, clownfish, angelfish, and sea snakes.

- Koala Reef – A shallow reef great for beginners, with abundant corals and fish life.

- Beatrice Rock – Known for its strong currents, making it ideal for drift diving and seeing large schools of fish.

Deep & Advanced Dive Sites

- Kirby’s Rock – A deep dive site with black coral, whitetip sharks, and large groupers.

- Bahura (Merriel’s Rock) – A drift diving hotspot filled with soft corals and big fish.

- Daryl Laut Wreck – A sunken fishing boat covered in coral and teeming with marine life.

- Mapating Rock ("Shark Point") – One of Anilao’s most challenging dive sites, known for strong currents and occasional sightings of sharks.

- Sepok Wall – A vertical drop-off with gorgonian sea fans, reef sharks, and large pelagic fish.

Exciting Night Dive Sites

- Secret Bay – Transforms at night with bioluminescent plankton, bobtail squid, and rare night creatures.

- Anilao Pier – One of the best night diving spots for macro lovers.

- Basura – Offers incredible critter encounters at night, including blue-ringed octopuses.

 With over 40 dive sites, Anilao is a diver’s paradise for both beginners and experienced divers!
