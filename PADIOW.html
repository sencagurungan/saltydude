PADI Open Water Certification

The PADI Open Water Diver course is the world’s most popular scuba certification and your gateway to unforgettable diving adventures. Whether you're a beginner or looking to take your skills to the next level, this course will teach you everything you need to know to dive safely and enjoyably.

What’s Included?

✔ Classroom & Theory Sessions – Learn diving principles, safety rules, and underwater communication.

✔ Confined Water Training – Practice basic scuba skills in a controlled environment.

✔ Open Water Dives – Complete [X] training dives in Anilao’s beautiful dive sites.

✔ Full Gear Rental – Includes BCD, regulator, mask, fins, wetsuit, and tank.

✔ PADI Certification – Earn your official PADI Open Water Diver license (valid worldwide).

✔ Personalized Training – Small group or one-on-one instruction for maximum learning.

Prerequisites


Age Requirement: 10 years old and above

Swimming Ability: Basic swimming skills required

Medical Clearance: Must be in good health (a medical questionnaire will be provided)

After completing this course, you’ll be certified to dive up to 18 meters (60 feet) anywhere in the world!

Course Duration: Typically 3-4 days (flexible schedule available)

 Group Discounts Available – Perfect for friends and family!
